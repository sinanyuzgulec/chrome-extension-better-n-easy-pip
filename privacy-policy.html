Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Better & Easy PIP ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The extension only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Transparency settings: Your preferred video transparency level (10-100%)
  • Volume settings: Your preferred volume level (0-100%)
  • Language preference: Your chosen interface language (English or Turkish)

All of this data is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API, which is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Synced across your Chrome browsers if you're signed in (handled by Chrome, not by us)
  • Never sent to any external server
  • Never shared with third parties

Permissions We Use

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab and detect video elements
  • scripting: To inject scripts that control video transparency and volume
  • storage: To save your preferences locally on your device

Data Usage

The extension uses your settings data only to:

  • Apply your preferred transparency and volume settings to videos
  • Display the interface in your preferred language
  • Remember your preferences between browser sessions

Third-Party Services

The extension includes a link to Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com). This is only a link - clicking it will open the website in a new tab. We do not track whether you click this link or not.

Data Security

All settings are stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API. We do not have access to this data, and it is protected by Chrome's security features.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
